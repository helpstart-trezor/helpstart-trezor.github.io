Trezor Setup and Login Guide

Introduction

Welcome to our Trezor setup and login guide. Here, you'll learn how to securely set up and login to your Trezor device.

Step-by-Step Setup

  1. Visit the official Trezor website: trezor.io/start
  2. Download and install the Trezor Bridge software.
  3. Connect your Trezor device to your computer.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup.

Login to Your Trezor

  1. Open your Trezor Wallet application.
  2. Connect your Trezor device to your computer.
  3. Enter your PIN when prompted.
  4. Access your cryptocurrency securely.
